## Visiting Puerto Rico in December

Planning to visit Puerto Rico in December? Here's everything you need to know for a great solo trip.

## Weather in December

In December, Puerto Rico experiences cold and possibly snowy. Pack accordingly with winter clothing.

## Crowds & Prices

Expect high tourist crowds in December. Peak Season Prices apply during this time.

## What to Pack

Pack for winter weather with layers, comfortable shoes, and rain protection.

## Events & Festivals

3. Check weather forecasts before outdoor activities
