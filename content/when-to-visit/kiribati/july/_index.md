## Visiting Kiribati in July

Planning to visit Kiribati in July? Here's everything you need to know for a great solo trip.

In July, Kiribati experiences cold and possibly snowy. Pack accordingly with winter clothing.

Expect high tourist crowds in July. Peak Season Prices apply during this time.
