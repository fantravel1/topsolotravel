## Visiting Cuba in December

Planning to visit Cuba in December? Here's everything you need to know for a great solo trip.

## Weather in December

In December, Cuba experiences cold and possibly snowy. Pack accordingly with winter clothing.

## Crowds & Prices

Expect high tourist crowds in December. Peak Season Prices apply during this time.

## What to Pack

Pack for winter weather with layers, comfortable shoes, and rain protection.

## Events & Festivals

Check local listings for festivals and events happening in Cuba during December.

## Best Activities in December

Winter activities are ideal in December. Outdoor activities depend on weather conditions.

## Solo Travel Tips for December

1. Book accommodations early if visiting during high season
2. Join group tours to meet other travelers
3. Check weather forecasts before outdoor activities
