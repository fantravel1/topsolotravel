## The Best Places to Travel Solo in 2025

Our expert picks for solo travelers this year, based on safety ratings, value for money, ease of solo travel, and unique experiences.

### 1. Japan

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Exceptionally safe, incredible food scene with solo-friendly dining, efficient transport, and a culture that respects privacy while remaining welcoming.

**Best for**: First-time solo travelers, food lovers, culture enthusiasts

**Budget**: $80-150/day

---

### 2. Portugal

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Europe's most affordable western destination, warm locals, thriving digital nomad scene, and excellent safety ratings.

**Best for**: Budget travelers, digital nomads, beach lovers

**Budget**: $60-120/day

---

### 3. New Zealand

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Stunning natural beauty, extremely safe, well-organized tourist infrastructure, and friendly locals.

**Best for**: Adventure seekers, nature lovers, outdoor enthusiasts

**Budget**: $100-180/day

---

### 4. Thailand

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Budget-friendly, established backpacker trail, easy to meet other travelers, incredible food.

**Best for**: Budget travelers, first-timers, social travelers

**Budget**: $30-80/day

---

### 5. Iceland

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: One of the world's safest countries, stunning landscapes, organized tours make solo exploration easy.

**Best for**: Nature lovers, adventure seekers, photographers

**Budget**: $150-250/day

---

### 6. Taiwan

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Extremely safe, affordable, incredible street food scene, efficient public transport, and welcoming culture.

**Best for**: Food lovers, budget travelers, culture enthusiasts

**Budget**: $50-100/day

---

### 7. Slovenia

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Compact size, stunning nature, affordable for Europe, safe, and easy to navigate.

**Best for**: Nature lovers, budget European travelers, outdoor enthusiasts

**Budget**: $70-120/day

---

### 8. Colombia

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Transformed safety situation, incredible value, warm locals, diverse landscapes.

**Best for**: Adventure seekers, budget travelers, social travelers

**Budget**: $40-80/day

---

### 9. Scotland

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Safe, English-speaking, stunning landscapes, excellent solo hiking opportunities.

**Best for**: History buffs, nature lovers, whisky enthusiasts

**Budget**: $80-150/day

---

### 10. Vietnam

**Why it's perfect for solo travelers**: Incredibly affordable, delicious food, easy backpacker trail, rich culture.

**Best for**: Budget travelers, food lovers, adventure seekers

**Budget**: $30-60/day
