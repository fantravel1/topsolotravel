## Why These Experiences?

We didn't choose famous destinations. We chose **moments that are better alone**.

These experiences share common traits: they reward patience (hard with companions), require personal processing (impossible in conversation), or offer freedom that evaporates in groups.

Could you watch Angkor Wat sunrise with a partner? Sure. But you'd talk through it, photograph each other, plan breakfast. Alone, you might cry. You might sit for three hours. You might have a spiritual experience you never discuss but never forget.

**That's what this list is about.**
