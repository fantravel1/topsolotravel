## Where to Stay When Traveling Alone

Your accommodation sets the tone for your solo trip. Want to meet people? Choose a social hostel. Need privacy and comfort? Find a boutique hotel without single supplements. Our guides help you find the perfect stay for your travel style.

### What You'll Find Here

- **Hostel guides** featuring the most social and solo-friendly properties
- **Hotel recommendations** without single supplements
- **Boutique stays** with great common areas for meeting people
- **Safety tips** for staying secure as a solo traveler
- **Booking strategies** to avoid paying extra for traveling alone

### Accommodation Types

**Hostels** - Social atmospheres, budget-friendly, great for meeting people

**Boutique Hotels** - Private rooms with social common areas

**Guesthouses & B&Bs** - Local hospitality and insider tips

**Capsule Hotels** - Private sleeping pods at hostel prices (Japan specialty)

**Aparthotels** - Your own space priced per unit, not per person

### Popular Solo Travel Stays

- **Generator Hostels** - Stylish hostels across Europe
- **Selina** - Digital nomad-friendly co-living spaces
- **Wombats** - Award-winning hostel chain
- **Japanese Business Hotels** - Perfect for solo travelers in Japan
