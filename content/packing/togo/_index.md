## Solo Travel Packing List for Togo

Packing smart for Togo means traveling light while being prepared. Here's your complete packing guide.

## Essential Items

Never leave without these crucial items that are hard to replace while traveling.

## Clothing

Pack versatile pieces suitable for Togo's tropical to arid climate. Layers are key.

## Electronics & Gadgets

Keep electronics minimal but functional. A universal adapter is essential.

## Toiletries

Pack travel-sized items. Most toiletries are available locally if needed.

## Documents & Money

Keep digital and physical copies of all important documents.

## Nice to Have

Nice to have but not essential - decide based on your travel style.

## What to Leave at Home

Save space and reduce risk by leaving these items at home.

## Packing Tips for Solo Travelers

1. Pack light - you'll carry everything yourself
2. Choose versatile items that mix and match
3. Roll clothes to save space
4. Keep valuables in your carry-on
5. Leave room for souvenirs
