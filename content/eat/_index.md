## Eating Solo: A Solo Traveler's Guide

Dining alone is one of the great pleasures of solo travel - no compromises, no waiting, just you and incredible food. Our guides help you find restaurants that welcome solo diners, from counter-seat ramen shops to tapas bars where eating alone feels natural.

### What You'll Find Here

- **Solo dining tips and strategies** for confident restaurant experiences
- **City guides** highlighting the best places for eating alone
- **Counter seating recommendations** at world-class restaurants
- **Street food and market guides** perfect for solo grazing
- **Cultural tips** for dining alone in different countries

Whether you're a first-time solo diner feeling nervous about eating alone, or an experienced traveler looking for the best counter seats in Tokyo, we've got you covered.

### Top Solo Dining Cities

- **Tokyo** - Counter culture capital
- **Barcelona** - Tapas bar perfection
- **Singapore** - Hawker center heaven
- **Lisbon** - Tasca bar tradition
- **Bangkok** - Street food paradise
