## Your Day in Seoul Awaits

Seoul is a city of contrasts held together by remarkable efficiency. A 600-year-old palace and a K-pop billboard share the same view. A traditional tea house has better WiFi than most Western hotels. The future arrived here early, but it didn't bulldoze the past.

Solo travelers fit seamlessly into Seoul's rhythm. The pojangmacha stall, the noraebang room, the jjimjilbang floor ‚Äî all designed for individuals as much as groups. Korea understands that alone doesn't mean lonely.

Put on the hanbok. Cross the palace gates. Seoul has been waiting.
