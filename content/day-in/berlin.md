## Your Day in Berlin Awaits

Berlin doesn't care about your schedule, your appearance, or your relationship status. It cares about whether you're present, curious, and open.

This is a city built from destruction and division, now unified by an anything-goes creativity. The Wall is gone; only art and memory remain. The clubs don't close; neither do the conversations.

Come as you are. Berlin will meet you there.
