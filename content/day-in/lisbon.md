## Your Day in Lisbon Awaits

Lisbon is a city that asks you to slow down. The hills force it. The afternoon wine encourages it. The saudade seduces it.

As a solo traveler, you'll find a city perfectly scaled for wandering alone. Tiny tascas seat you at the bar with regulars. Miradouros give you space to think. Fado houses don't care if you cry ‚Äî they expect it.

The light here is different. Atlantic gold filtered through sea mist, bouncing off tiles that tell stories, landing on your face as you climb yet another hill wondering what's at the top. The answer is always: something beautiful.
