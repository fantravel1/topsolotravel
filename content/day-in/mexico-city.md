## Your Day in CDMX Awaits

Mexico City is overwhelming in the best way ‚Äî a megalopolis of 21 million people that somehow feels like a collection of villages. Each neighborhood has its own rhythm, its own tacos, its own reason to exist.

Solo travelers find something particular here: a food culture that feeds individuals beautifully, a hospitality that extends to strangers, and a creative energy that makes every day feel like discovery.

The tacos are waiting. The mezcal is poured. Mexico City doesn't do things small, but it does them well.
