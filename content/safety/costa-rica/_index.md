## Is Costa Rica Safe for Solo Travelers?

Costa Rica is generally safe with normal precautions for solo travelers visiting the country.

## Safety Overview

Overall, Costa Rica presents manageable safety considerations for prepared travelers. Standard precautions help ensure a smooth trip.

## Solo Female Traveler Safety

Women traveling alone in Costa Rica generally report feeling safe. Respecting local customs and dressing appropriately helps.

## Emergency Contacts

Keep local emergency numbers saved in your phone. Your embassy can assist with serious issues.

## Health & Medical

Consult your doctor before travel. Ensure routine vaccinations are current and consider destination-specific recommendations.

## Transportation Safety

Use official transportation services. Ride-sharing apps offer transparency and safety. Avoid unlicensed vehicles.

## Final Safety Tips

1. Register with your embassy
2. Purchase travel insurance
3. Keep digital copies of important documents
4. Stay connected with family/friends
5. Trust your instincts
